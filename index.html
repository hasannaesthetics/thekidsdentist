Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

"What is a paediatric dentist?"

A paediatric dentist is a specialist who has completed additional postgraduate training in the dental care of infants, children and adolescents.

Read More →

"When should my child first see a dentist?"

The Kids Dentist recommends every child visits the dentist by his/her 1st birthday, as advocated by the American Academy of Pediatric Dentistry.

Read More →

"Why is primary or 'baby' teeth so important?"

Primary teeth are essential for chewing, speaking and guiding permanent teeth into the correct position. Healthy baby teeth lead to healthy adult teeth.
