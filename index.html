Common Questions

Quick Answers for Parents

When should my child first see a dentist?

We recommend scheduling your child's first visit by their first birthday or within 6 months of their first tooth erupting, as advocated by the American Academy of Pediatric Dentistry.

What age group do you treat?

We specialize in treating infants, children, and adolescents from birth through teenage years. We also have expertise in treating children with special needs.

Do you handle dental emergencies?

Yes! We provide emergency care for knocked-out teeth, fractures, and urgent dental issues. Call us immediately if your child experiences dental trauma.
