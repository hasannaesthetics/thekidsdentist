Our Services

Comprehensive pediatric dental care tailored to your child's unique needs, from preventive treatments to complex procedures.

🛡️

Preventive Care

Prevention is the foundation of excellent oral health. Our comprehensive preventive care program helps protect your child's teeth from cavities and establishes healthy habits that last a lifetime.

  • ✓

    Regular Check-ups & Cleanings

    Professional teeth cleaning and comprehensive oral examinations every 6 months to detect and prevent dental problems early.

  • ✓

    Fluoride Application

    Fluoride varnish or Silver Diamine Fluoride (SDF) treatments to strengthen tooth enamel and prevent decay.

  • ✓

    Pit and Fissure Sealants

    Protective coating applied to the chewing surfaces of back teeth to prevent cavities in hard-to-clean grooves.

  • ✓

    Oral Hygiene Education

    Age-appropriate instruction on proper brushing, flossing, and dietary habits for optimal oral health.

✨

Restorative Treatment

When decay or damage occurs, we use the latest materials and techniques to restore your child's teeth to full function and natural appearance.

  • ✓

    White Fillings (Mercury-Free)

    Tooth-colored composite fillings that blend seamlessly with natural teeth, containing no mercury for maximum safety.

  • ✓

    Aesthetic White Zirconia Crowns

    Premium tooth-colored crowns that provide strength and durability while maintaining a natural, beautiful appearance.

  • ✓

    Stainless Steel Crowns

    Durable, pre-formed crowns for baby teeth that require extensive restoration, ideal for back teeth.

  • ✓

    Cavity Risk Assessment

    Comprehensive evaluation to identify risk factors and develop personalized prevention strategies.

🦷

Root Canal Treatment for Baby Teeth

When tooth decay reaches the nerve of a baby tooth, specialized root canal treatment can save the tooth and avoid premature loss that could affect jaw development and permanent tooth alignment.

  • ✓

    Pulpotomy

    Removal of infected pulp from the crown portion of the tooth while preserving healthy pulp in the roots. Often called a "baby root canal."

  • ✓

    Pulpectomy

    Complete removal of infected pulp from both crown and roots when decay has spread throughout the tooth's nerve system.

Why Save Baby Teeth?

  • Maintain proper spacing for permanent teeth
  • Support normal jaw development
  • Preserve chewing function and nutrition
  • Prevent speech development issues
  • Avoid costly orthodontic problems later
💤

Sedation Options

For anxious children or those requiring extensive treatment, we offer safe, effective sedation options to ensure a stress-free, comfortable dental experience.

  • ✓

    Nitrous Oxide ("Laughing Gas")

    Mild sedative gas that helps children relax while remaining conscious and responsive. Effects wear off quickly after treatment.

  • ✓

    Oral Sedation

    Medication administered by mouth before the appointment to reduce anxiety and increase cooperation during treatment.

  • ✓

    General Anesthesia (GA)

    Complete sedation in a hospital setting for children requiring extensive dental work or those with severe dental anxiety or special needs.

When Sedation May Be Recommended

  • Severe dental anxiety or phobia
  • Very young children requiring extensive treatment
  • Children with special needs or medical conditions
  • Multiple procedures in one visit
  • Traumatic dental injuries requiring immediate treatment
😁

Orthodontic Services

Early orthodontic assessment and intervention can prevent more serious problems and reduce the need for extensive treatment later.

  • ✓

    Early Orthodontic Assessment

    Evaluation of jaw growth and tooth development to identify potential issues that benefit from early intervention.

  • ✓

    Removable Appliances

    Space maintainers and other appliances to guide proper tooth eruption and jaw development.

  • ✓

    Habit Correction

    Treatment to address thumb-sucking, tongue-thrusting, and other oral habits that affect tooth alignment.

Benefits of Early Orthodontic Intervention

  • Guide jaw growth in optimal direction
  • Create space for proper tooth eruption
  • Reduce risk of impacted permanent teeth
  • Improve facial symmetry and aesthetics
  • Potentially reduce need for tooth extractions
  • Shorter treatment time with braces later
⚡

Emergency Dental Care

Dental emergencies can happen anytime. We're here to provide immediate care for knocked-out teeth, fractures, and other urgent dental situations.

  • ✓

    Knocked-Out Teeth

    Immediate treatment to save and re-implant permanent teeth, with the best outcomes when treated within 30 minutes.

  • ✓

    Fractured Teeth Management

    Restoration of chipped, cracked, or broken teeth to prevent infection and restore function and appearance.

  • ✓

    Dental Pain Relief

    Quick diagnosis and treatment of toothaches, abscesses, and other sources of dental pain.

  • ✓

    Soft Tissue Injuries

    Treatment for cuts to lips, cheeks, tongue, and gums often associated with dental trauma.

What to Do in a Dental Emergency

  • Call us immediately at 6235 7279
  • For knocked-out permanent teeth, place back in socket if possible
  • Keep tooth moist in milk or saliva
  • Apply cold compress for swelling
  • Seek treatment within 30 minutes for best outcomes

Sports Dentistry & Mouthguards

Protect your child's smile during sports and physical activities with custom-fitted mouthguards designed for maximum comfort and protection.

  • ✓

    Custom Mouthguards

    Professionally designed and fitted mouthguards that provide superior protection compared to store-bought options.

  • ✓

    Sports Injury Prevention

    Education on protecting teeth during contact sports, skating, cycling, and other activities.

  • ✓

    Comfortable Fit

Why Custom Mouthguards?

  • Prevent tooth fractures and knockouts

  • Protect orthodontic work

  • Better fit means better protection
  • Available in fun colors and designs
