Comprehensive Child Dental Care

Our Services

The Kids Dentist not only abides by its motto of "fun dentistry for kids", but provides comprehensive oral healthcare for children of all ages, from birth to 18 years.

At The Kids Dentist, we offer a range of dental services for our young patients: from oral disease prevention such as fluoride therapy to restorative treatment such as white fillings and aesthetic white zirconia crowns. Children who are highly anxious or require extensive dental treatment may benefit from having treatment under laughing gas sedation or general anaesthesia.

Aesthetic white zirconia crowns of the upper front four teeth
"Laughing gas" sedation for anxious children
Gum boil arising from a decayed, infected tooth
Traumatised, fractured permanent teeth in a young child

Category 01

Behaviour Modification & Pain Management

Our team of dentists are specially trained and equipped to help anxious children feel safe and comfortable during dental treatment. Our clinic is designed for children and our staff are patient, caring and most importantly, love being with kids.

  • ✓
    Behaviour modification and shaping
    Child-friendly techniques to build confidence and reduce anxiety over time.
  • ✓
    Computerised local anaesthesia delivery
    Sensor-controlled system to minimise injection discomfort — no traditional needle anxiety.
  • ✓
    Oral sedation & laughing gas (nitrous oxide)
    Safe, effective sedation to help anxious children relax. No IV sedation used.
  • ✓
    General anaesthesia
    For extremely anxious children; performed only by well-qualified paediatric anaesthetists.

Category 02

Restorative Dentistry

When cavities or dental damage occur, our specialist team provides gentle, effective restorative treatment using the latest techniques and tooth-coloured materials that blend naturally with your child's smile.

  • ✓
    Paediatric crowns (white zirconia & stainless steel)
    Recommended for extensive damage; generally outlast traditional fillings.
  • ✓
    Root canal treatment (pulpotomy & pulpectomy) for baby teeth
    Saves badly decayed or infected baby teeth from extraction.
  • ✓
    Leong's premolars (dens evaginatus)
    Preventive or revascularisation treatment for protruded premolar biting surface.
  • ✓
    White fillings — mercury-free
    No amalgam. Safe for patients, staff and the environment.
Book Restorative Treatment

Category 03

Dental Trauma & Sports Dentistry

Accidents happen. Whether your child has chipped, broken, or knocked out a tooth, our team is experienced in managing dental trauma promptly and effectively to achieve the best possible outcome.

  • ✓
    Management of fractured teeth
    Immediate and long-term management including treatment of abscess and discolouration.
  • ✓
    Knocked-out tooth (avulsion) management
    Act fast — replanting within the hour gives the best outcome.
  • ✓
    Sports mouthguards
    Customised mouthguards for children participating in contact sports.
Book an Emergency Appointment

Category 04

Preventive & Minimal Intervention Dentistry

Prevention is the cornerstone of our practice. We believe that a lifetime of healthy smiles begins with good habits established early. Our preventive services protect your child's teeth before problems arise.

  • ✓ Silver Diamine Fluoride — topical arrest of decay progression
  • ✓ Home and professional fluoride therapies — customised to each child's decay risk
  • ✓ Pit and fissure sealants — prevent decay in newly erupted permanent molars
  • ✓ Removable orthodontics — simple retainer-type braces for anterior crossbite
  • ✓ Space maintainers — preserve arch space after premature tooth loss
  • ✓ Habit-breaking appliances — for thumb-sucking or pacifier habits
