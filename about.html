Our Story

Fun Dentistry for Kids

Founded in 2006, The Kids Dentist was established as Singapore's very first dedicated pediatric dentistry practice. Our mission was simple yet revolutionary: to create a dental practice exclusively focused on children's unique needs, transforming the dental experience from something children feared into something they genuinely enjoyed.

At The Kids Dentist, we abide by our motto: Fun dentistry for kids. We are a dedicated team of specialist paediatric dentists and dental support staff providing specialized dentistry for infants, children, and adolescents in Singapore. We also attend to children with special needs and those with complex medical conditions.

Located at the prestigious Camden Medical Centre in Orchard, our practice combines clinical excellence with a warm, child-friendly environment. Every aspect of our practice—from our colorful waiting area to our specially trained staff—is designed with children in mind.

We are committed to ensuring that your child has a positive dental experience each time they visit us. Our approach goes beyond just treating teeth; we build relationships with our young patients, helping them develop healthy oral habits that will last a lifetime.

👨‍⚕️ Dr. Rashid Tahir Photo

Dr. Rashid Tahir

Specialist Paediatric Dentist | Founding President, Society for Paediatric Dentistry (Singapore)

BDS (Singapore) 1995 MSc Children's Dentistry (London) 1998 - Distinction 3M Dental Postgraduate Prize

Dr. Rashid Tahir is a highly accomplished pediatric dentist who has dedicated his entire career to providing specialized oral health care for children since 1997. With over 25 years of experience, he is recognized as one of Singapore's leading authorities in pediatric dentistry.

Dr. Rashid completed his graduate training at the prestigious University of London in 1998, where he was conferred a Master of Science with Distinction in Children's Dentistry (Paedodontics). His exceptional academic performance earned him the 3M Dental Postgraduate Prize for being the best student in his cohort.

From 2002 to 2006, Dr. Rashid served as an Assistant Professor at the Faculty of Dentistry, National University of Singapore (NUS), and as a consultant at the National University Hospital Dental Centre. His passion for teaching continues today as an Adjunct Associate Professor at NUS, where he shapes the next generation of dental professionals.

Beyond clinical practice, Dr. Rashid has held numerous prestigious positions in professional organizations. He is the founding President of the Society for Paediatric Dentistry (Singapore) and served as President of the Pediatric Dentistry Association of Asia (2016-2018). He was also Chairman of the Specialist Accreditation Committee for Paediatric Dentistry (2008-2015) and continues to serve as a Member of the Dental Specialists Accreditation Board.

A proud father of two beautiful young daughters, Dr. Rashid brings both professional expertise and genuine parental understanding to his practice. His personal mission is to make dentistry fun for kids while providing the highest standard of specialized care.

👩‍⚕️ Dr. Sylvia Koh Photo

Dr. Sylvia Koh

Specialist Paediatric Dentist

BDS Specialist in Paediatric Dentistry

Dr. Sylvia Koh is a dedicated specialist pediatric dentist with extensive experience in providing comprehensive dental care for children of all ages. Her gentle approach and exceptional clinical skills have made her a trusted partner for families throughout Singapore.

With advanced specialist training in pediatric dentistry, Dr. Koh excels in treating children with diverse needs, from infants experiencing their first dental visit to adolescents requiring complex orthodontic intervention. She has a particular expertise in working with children who have special needs and those with complex medical conditions, ensuring they receive compassionate, specialized care.

Dr. Koh's philosophy centers on building trust and making every child feel safe and comfortable. She takes time to explain procedures in age-appropriate language, turning potentially anxiety-inducing appointments into positive experiences. Her patient, understanding demeanor helps even the most nervous children relax in the dental chair.

As an integral part of The Kids Dentist team, Dr. Koh is committed to preventive care and early intervention. She works closely with parents to establish good oral hygiene habits from an early age, setting the foundation for a lifetime of healthy smiles.

Areas of Expertise

  • Treatment of children with complex medical conditions
  • Preventive care and early intervention
  • Behavior management and anxiety reduction
  • Age-appropriate communication with young patients
  • Restorative treatments including crowns and fillings
